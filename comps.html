Equations for Radiative Properties


  • Albedo: $\alpha(\lambda) = \dfrac{F^\uparrow(\lambda)}{F^\downarrow(\lambda)}$
  • Transmittance: $T(\lambda) = \dfrac{F^\downarrow_{bot.}(\lambda)}{F^\downarrow_{top}(\lambda)}$
  • Reflectance: $R(\lambda) = \dfrac{F^\uparrow_{top}(\lambda)-F^\uparrow_{bot.}(\lambda)}{F^\downarrow_{top}(\lambda)}$
  • Absorptance: $A(\lambda) = \dfrac{(F^\downarrow_{top}(\lambda)-F^\uparrow_{top}(\lambda))-(F^\downarrow_{bot.}(\lambda)-F^\uparrow_{bot.}(\lambda))}{F^\downarrow_{top}(\lambda)}$
